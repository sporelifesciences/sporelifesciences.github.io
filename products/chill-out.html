Where are Spore products made?

All Spore products are made in Pennsylvania. All product development and processing occurs in a facility that is cGMP, FDA registered, USDA certified organic, Canada site licensed, EU site licensed, Kosher, and Halal accredited.

Where are Spore's mushroom ingredients sourced from?

All of our ingredients are carefully and sustainably sourced from rich ecosystems around the world. Every single ingredient is sourced from cGMP accredited suppliers to ensure that you get the highest quality product in each of our blends.

What are Spores responsible sourcing and harvesting practices?

Spore has established a standard audit process for each of our ingredient suppliers. Our quality assurance protocol ensures that every ingredient in our products meets the label claim and supporting guidelines.

What safety, purity, and efficacy standards does Spore uphold?

All Spore ingredients are ethically and sustainably sourced from cGMP accredited suppliers.We then audit and run 3rd party lab testing to ensure that our products contain ingredients of the highest quality, purity, and efficacy.

Why should I take a blend vs. one functional mushroom?

At Spore, were focused on optimizing every aspect of your overall health and wellness. Intelligently formulated blends offer a much richer nutrient profile than one functional mushroom on its own. Each of our blends was carefully designed to maximize your results and get your health right where you want it to be.

Why do some of your blends contain rice flour?

Spore uses rice flour as an excipient, which is commonly used in supplement formulations to stabilize and support the bioavailability of each ingredient. This excipient ensures you will get the optimal results from each of our powerful formulations.

How did you come up with your blends?

Our formulation team spent years reviewing clinical studies, researching medical breakthroughs, and closely monitoring the results of various natural ingredients to build our unique and powerful blends. What is a cGMP?

A cGMP is a system that ensures products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards. The c in cGMP stands for current, which indicates that the most recent standards, technology, and methods are being applied to all operations. All Spore products are manufactured following these strict regulations, in an NSF cGMP-accredited facility.

Do you test your products for heavy metals?

Yes, all of our products are subject to 3rd party testing for heavy metals and other contaminants. and meet and exceed industry standards. All our products are formulated in an NSF cGMP-certified facility, so we can ensure our products and our mushrooms are of the highest quality while meeting all industry standards.

Are functional mushrooms legal?

Yes! Functional mushrooms have been used for over 5,000 years as a cornerstone of many medicinal treatments and medical systems. Dont worry none of the mushrooms in our blends are psychoactive or illegal.

What is extraction?

Extraction is a method used to pull beneficial compounds, vitamins, and minerals from a raw material in our case, functional mushrooms. All of our mushrooms are extracted using single or dual solvent extraction methods, with water as our main solvent. Proper extraction allows us to ensure accurate dosing and effectiveness in all of our products, so you can bring the full power of functional mushrooms into your daily routine.

Are Spore products appropriate for vegetarians or vegans?

Yes! Spore products are vegetarian and vegan-friendly, containing no animal-based ingredients.

Are Spore products gluten-free?

Yes, Spore products are gluten-free and safe for those with gluten allergies. We strive to not only adhere to all FDA "Gluten Free" labeling guidelines and standards, but to go above the minimum requirements. Every production batch is tested at a 10ppm gluten threshold level, well below the current FDA guidelines of less than 20ppm gluten for gluten-free product labeling. In addition, we test every batch thats received and will reject it if any gluten-grain contamination is found.

Why are Spore products blended with other ingredients such as green tea, turmeric, ashwagandha, ginkgo biloba, chamomile, passion flower, etc.?

While functional mushrooms are at the heart of our blends, their effects can be amplified with complementary natural ingredients. All of the ingredients used in our products have been thoroughly researched and proven their efficacy in clinical studies. The result is potent functional mushroom formulas that offer the nutrients you need to thrive.

Are Spore supplement capsules suitable for a Paleo/Keto diet?

Yes, Spore capsules and functional mushrooms are suitable for both Paleo and Ketogenic diets.

Are your capsules flavored?

Our capsules are unflavored, but you may notice a tinge of pleasant earthiness when you take them.

What are functional mushrooms and why should I take them?

Functional mushrooms are a subset of the mushroom family that contain a rich, potent nutrient profile. These mushrooms are loaded with antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals and have been used for generations as elixirs to improve overall health. They also provide beneficial amino acids/proteins and compounds like triterpenes (Turkey Tail & Reishi), neurotrophic factors (Lion's Mane), and cordycepin (from Cordyceps) all of which contribute to improved mental health, immunity, and performance.

What are nootropics?

Nootropics are natural ingredients that have beneficial effects on brain function, including boosting memory, motivation, alertness, improving general cognitive ability and creativity, and may reduce age-related decline in brain function.

When is the best time of day to take Spore supplements?

There is no specific guidance on the best time of day to consume Spore supplements. Each person processes and metabolizes the ingredients differently. However, when youre taking a blend for a specific purpose, you should adhere to the directions on the product. For example, our mushroom blends with Cordyceps are known to increase energy and would be best taken in the morning. Other Spore blends have a more calming effect and would be best taken in the evening. For further guidance, please consult your health practitioner.

When will I notice a difference in my body and mind?

The honest answer is: it depends. The processing and metabolization rate of functional mushrooms varies from person to person. However, a Spore blend focused on energy or relaxation can typically be noticed quickly, while a blend focused on immunity offers ongoing "behind the scenes" benefits by protecting and defending your immune system.

Can I take Spore products if I have a mold allergy?

Yes, none of our mushrooms are known to produce mycotoxins like black mold and other imperfect fungi.

Do Spore mushroom blends encourage yeast growth in the body?

A common misconception is that eating mushrooms facilitates fungal overgrowth in the body, but has been proven false. Not only are mushrooms very low in the sugars that promote yeast growth, but they also strengthen the body's immune response. If you're currently experiencing yeast overgrowth you should consult a healthcare professional about your specific situation.

Can I take multiple products safely?

Yes. Each mushroom species has unique nutritional benefits and you can multiply the benefits by combining more than one species. Like all dietary supplements, check with your health care provider for any questions related to diet.

Can Spore products be taken by children or women who are pregnant or nursing?

We recommend that you consult your healthcare professional before consuming a Spore product if you are pregnant, nursing, or planning to give a supplement to children.

What is the shelf life of your products?

All of our products have a two-year stable shelf life from the manufacturing date, listed on your bottle.

How do I store my Spore products?

Spore products should always be stored at room temperature. No refrigeration or freezing is required.

Can I take Spore products with other medications?

While functional mushrooms are a natural product, as with any dietary supplement, we always recommend consulting your health care provider if you have questions regarding diet or complications with existing medications.

Will the mushrooms encourage yeast to grow in my body?

If you have Candida or any other medical condition you should always consult with your health care provider before introducing mushrooms or other dietary supplements into your diet.
